Introduction

Is there anyone you know who has been acting annoying lately? Maybe they've been bugging you about
something, or they're poking you in an attempt to get you to unleash your rage in front of everyone? If so
then they're probably a killer bunnie who is trying to study you for your weaknesses so the killer bunnies
can take over the world.

Killer bunnies are small, pink, mutant Colar bunnies that have turned evil. They believe to have evolved
inside the sewer system of the grandpa prison. From there, they teamed up with the quackers and broke
out of prison, all with the help of poisoned apple juice. They then broke into the apple juice factory and
poisoned the supply, making off with the last fresh apple juice. Then they fed the apple juice to the cloning
machine to create more killer bunnies to help rule the world.

From then on the killer bunny numbers have been greatly increasing, and there is at least one killer bunny
in every public space in the world. Of course, if the killer bunnies were in their true form they would be
much easier to identify. Killer bunnies have a mutant ability that allows them to change their appearance
to a human child (Also rarely an adult human).
